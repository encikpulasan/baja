QL Eco Green Organomineral Soil Conditioner is an innovative class of engineered amendment specifically designed to improve soil health. It incorporates both organic and mineral components to provide substantial agricultural benefits, including improved soil structure, enhanced nutrient availability, and better water retention.

Our soil conditioner contains a comprehensive nutrient profile including:

  • • Primary macronutrients: Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K)
  • • Secondary macronutrients: Calcium (Ca), Magnesium (Mg), Sulfur (S)
  • • Essential micronutrients: Boron, Chlorine, Copper, Iron, Manganese, Molybdenum, Nickel, Zinc
  • • Organic matter and organic carbon for soil health

We offer our products in multiple forms to suit different application needs:

  • • Powder form (most common) - Dark brown color
  • • Granular form - For easy broadcasting
  • • Liquid concentrate - For precise application and foliar feeding

Application rates vary based on soil conditions and crop requirements:

  • • Low Application: 0.25-0.5 kg/ha (light conditioning and maintenance)
  • • Medium Application: 0.5-1.25 kg/ha (recommended for optimal results)
  • • High Application: 1.25-2.5 kg/ha (intensive soil rehabilitation)

Consult with our agricultural specialists for customized recommendations based on your specific conditions.

The optimal timing for application depends on your farming cycle:

  • • Pre-planting: Apply 2-4 weeks before planting for soil preparation
  • • During planting: Mix with soil at planting time
  • • Growing season: Side-dress application during active growth
  • • Post-harvest: Apply after harvest to maintain soil health

Proper storage ensures product effectiveness:

  • • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • • Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use
  • • Avoid exposure to moisture and extreme temperatures
  • • Use within 2 years of purchase for optimal results
  • • Keep away from children and livestock

Our soil conditioner works through multiple mechanisms:

  • • Soil Structure: Improves aggregation and porosity for better root penetration
  • • Water Retention: Increases water holding capacity and reduces runoff
  • • Nutrient Availability: Enhances CEC and nutrient uptake efficiency
  • • Microbial Activity: Promotes beneficial soil microorganisms
  • • pH Balance: Helps maintain optimal soil pH levels

Yes, our soil conditioner is compatible with most fertilizers and can actually enhance their effectiveness. However, we recommend:

  • • Reducing chemical fertilizer use by 20-30% when using our products
  • • Conducting a soil test before combining products
  • • Consulting with our technical team for specific fertilizer combinations
  • • Monitoring plant response and adjusting accordingly

QL Eco Green products are effective across various soil types:

  • • Clay soils: Improves drainage and reduces compaction
  • • Sandy soils: Increases water and nutrient retention
  • • Loamy soils: Maintains and enhances existing soil quality
  • • Acidic soils: Helps balance pH levels
  • • Alkaline soils: Improves nutrient availability
