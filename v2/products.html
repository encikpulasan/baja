Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS)

QL Eco-Green Organic Fertilizer MSDS

Safety and handling information for QL Eco Green organic fertilizers. Always follow recommended guidelines for safe use and storage.

Product Identification

  • Product Name: QL Eco-Green Organic Fertilizer
  • Type: Powder, Pellet, Hybrid Pellet
  • Use: Organic fertilizer for agriculture, horticulture, landscaping

Hazards Identification

  • Not classified as hazardous under normal use.
  • Non-toxic, non-flammable, non-corrosive.
  • May cause mild irritation to eyes or skin on prolonged contact.

Composition / Information on Ingredients

  • 100% organic matter (fermented agricultural waste, EM microbes)
  • No chemical additives or synthetic substances

First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air if dust is inhaled.
  • Skin Contact: Wash with soap and water if irritation occurs.
  • Eye Contact: Rinse with clean water for several minutes.
  • Ingestion: Rinse mouth, seek medical advice if unwell.

Fire Fighting Measures

  • Not flammable. Use water spray, foam, or dry chemical for surrounding fire.

Accidental Release Measures

  • Sweep up and reuse or dispose as non-hazardous waste.
  • Avoid creating dust. Use PPE if necessary.

Handling & Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area.
  • Keep bags sealed when not in use.
  • Use gloves and mask if handling large quantities.

Other Information

  • Product is biodegradable and environmentally safe.
  • For agricultural use only. Not for human or animal consumption.
