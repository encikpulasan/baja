Our Journey

23 Years of Malaysian Innovation

From QL Resources Berhad's vision to QL Eco Green's advanced bio-fermentation technology leadership.

1987

QL Resources Founded

QL Resources Berhad was established as an integrated agro-based business group with the vision to create nourishing products from agro resources. QL stands for "quan li" (全利) meaning "Benefits for All".

2001

QL Eco Green Established

QL Poultry Farms Sdn Bhd (QL Eco Green) was incorporated, starting as a manufacturer and developing into one of Malaysia's leading organic fertilizer producers through advanced bio-fermentation technology.

2012

Innovation Recognition

QL Resources won the Green Leadership Award 2012 by the Asia Responsible Entrepreneurship Awards. Dr. Chia Song Kun was bestowed with the Malaysian Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year 2012.

2015

ASEAN Recognition

QL Resources was selected as the ASEAN Winner for the 6th ASEAN Business Awards 2015 under the Priority Integration Sector (PIS) – Fisheries category, recognizing excellence in agro-based business operations.

2018

Environmental Excellence

Received the "Outstanding Environmental Friendly Farm" award, recognizing our commitment to sustainable organic farming practices and advanced fermentation technology with 21-day heat treatment process.

2024

Continuing Innovation

Today, QL Eco Green continues to lead Malaysian organic fertilizer innovation with SIRIM-certified products, serving over 1,000 farmers and achieving remarkable yield increases across rice, palm oil, vegetables, and fruits.
