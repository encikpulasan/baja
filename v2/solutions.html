Key Benefits

Why Choose QL Eco Green Organic Fertilizer?

Discover the comprehensive advantages of our SIRIM-certified 100% EM organic fertilizer that makes it superior to chemical alternatives for Malaysian farming.

Cost Effective Solution

Significantly cheaper than chemical fertilizers with superior functionality. Our EM microorganisms transform chemical fertilizers into available forms, minimizing nutrient leaching by 50-70%.

  • Reduces chemical fertilizer usage
  • Minimizes nutrient leaching
  • Long-term cost savings

Superior Soil Conditioning

Transforms soil to become loose and friable, providing excellent tilth that encourages vigorous root hair growth and deeper, wider root zones for better plant development.

  • Improves soil structure
  • Enhances root development
  • Better water infiltration

Enhanced CEC Capacity

Increases Cation Exchange Capacity by up to 6 times higher than original levels, dramatically improving the soil's nutrient binding and water storage capacity.

  • 6x CEC improvement
  • Better nutrient retention
  • Enhanced water storage

Builds Organic Matter

Provides natural food source for beneficial soil microorganisms, enhancing their proliferation and conversion of inorganic elements into easily assimilated organic compounds.

  • 50-65% organic matter
  • Feeds beneficial microbes
  • Accelerates decomposition

Promotes Nitrogen Fixation

Contains beneficial microbes that fix nitrogen and convert phosphorus, potassium, and other elements into readily available compounds, reducing dependency on expensive chemical fertilizers.

  • Natural N-P-K conversion
  • Reduces chemical dependency
  • Substantial cost savings

Natural Enzymes & Probiotics

Beneficial microbes produce natural enzymes and probiotics that encourage growth-promoting substances and increase plant resistance against physiological diseases.

  • Natural enzyme production
  • Disease resistance boost
  • Growth enhancement

Rich in Organic Acids

Excellent source of organic acids that improve the assimilability and absorption of soil minerals by plants, enhancing overall nutrient uptake efficiency.

  • Improved mineral absorption
  • Enhanced nutrient uptake
  • Better plant nutrition

High Humus Content

Contains substantial humus that absorbs water up to 3 times its weight, helping soils retain moisture during droughts and providing beneficial humic acid for plant growth.

  • 3x water absorption
  • Drought resistance
  • Natural humic acid

Temperature Regulation

Minimizes sharp soil temperature fluctuations, reducing plant stress caused by wide gaps between daytime and nighttime temperatures, ensuring optimal growing conditions.

  • Reduces temperature stress
  • Stable growing environment
  • Better plant performance

Complete Soil Balance

Prolonged use leads to balanced soil pH (7.0-8.2) and achieves the ideal soil state: physically, chemically, and biologically balanced for maximum productivity.

Physical
Improved structure & drainage
Chemical
Optimal pH & nutrient balance
