QL Eco-Green organic fertilizer is made from 100% fermented agricultural waste using Effective Microorganisms (EM) technology. It contains no chemical additives or synthetic substances and is SIRIM MS 1517:2014 certified.

Yes, our organic fertilizer is suitable for vegetables, fruits, rice, oil palm, rubber, flowers, and herbs. It is safe for all soil types and crops, including organic-certified farms.

For best results, apply before planting as a base fertilizer and side-dress during crop growth. Recommended rates: 250-300g/m² for vegetables, 3-7kg/tree for oil palm, 500-750kg/hectare for rice. Consult our agronomists for crop-specific advice.

Yes, our fertilizer is odourless, pathogen-free, and safe to handle. It undergoes a 21-day heat treatment and EM fermentation, making it safe for workers and the environment. Use gloves and a mask if handling large quantities.
